    List of schedules

    Have a look

    A : Various forms & formats of letters for application of licensing.
    B : Rates of fee for test/analysis by the Central Drug Laboratory / Govt. Analyst.
    C : List of biological products & other special products whose import, sale, distribution & manufacturing are governed by special provision.
    C1 : Lists of special products.
    D : Lists of drugs EXEMPTED from the provision of import of drugs.
    E1 : (EVIL) Lists of poisonous substances (Ayurveda, Unani, Sidha) e.g. Snake Poison, Mercury.
    F: Minimum requirement for operating & running a Blood Bank.
    F1 : Lists of sera & vaccines ( Std.).
    F2 : Standards for surgical dressing.
    F3 : Standards for sterilized umbilical tapes.
    FF : Standards for ophthalmic preparations.
    G : Lists of drugs under medical supervisions. e.g.Anticancer drugs, Hormonal preparations etc.
    H : Lists of prescription drugs ( Rx labeled). e.g. Antidepressant, Analgesics, Antipyretics.
    J: Lists of Incurable Diseases ( Legally No drugs available) - HIV, Cancer, , Asthma etc.
    K : Drugs exempted from the provision of manufacturing.
    M : GMP for Pharmaceutical products.
    M1: GMP for Homoeopathic preparations.
    M2: GMP for Cosmetics.
    M3: GMP for Medical Devices
    N: (NEED)Minimum requirement for running a Pharmacy.
    O: Standards for disinfectant fluids. e.g. DettOl, Phenol, Lysol.
    P: Life periods & storage of drugs.
    P1: Pack size of drugs.
    Q: (COLOR) Lists of dyes & colors used in cosmetics and soaps.
    R: (RUBBER) Standards for Mechanical Contraceptives & Condoms of rubber latex.
    S: Standards for cosmetic preparations.
    T: GMP for Ayurvedic, Siddha, Unani medicines.
    U: Analytic records for drugs.
    V: Standards for patent/proprietary medicines.
    W: Lists of drugs which are to be manufactured under generic names only.
    X: Lists of Narcotic & Psychotic Substances. e.g. Barbitol, Amphetamine, etc.
    Y: Guidelines for Clinical Trials.

    Try to Have command over all this schedules this is important for any type of entrance exam
