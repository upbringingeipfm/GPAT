    Storage condition

    Cold temperature Not greater than 8°c
    Refrigerator Temp. Between 2-8°C
    Room Temp Prevailing in a working area
    Cool storage 8-25°c
    Warm storage 30-40°c
    Excessive heat above 40°c
    True density=Weight of powder/True volume of powder

    Determined by
  • Helium displacement method(Porous solid)
  • Liquid displacement method
  • Compressed method
    • Granule density=Granule Weight/Granule Volume

    Determined by
  • Mercury displacement method
    • Bulk density=Mass of a powder/Bulk Volume
    Tapped density=Mass of a powder/Tapped Volume
    Porosity=Bulk volume-True volume/Bulk Volume
    Porosity=True density-bulk density/True Density
    Hausner's ratio=Tapped density/Bulk density (pored density)
    % Compressibility/Carr's Index=Tapped-Bulk density/Tapped density

    Relationship between Angle of repose and powder flow

    Angle of repose flow
